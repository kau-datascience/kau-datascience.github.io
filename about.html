Interdisciplinary KAU Center of Applied Data Science and Innovation (KAU CADSI) at the Kiev Academic University (KAU). About KAU Center of Applied Data Science and Innovation

The project has a multi-purpose and interdisciplinary focus. The main tasks of the research work are the development of methods of science of data and their application to various branches of knowledge. Communication between the project participants who are representatives of the leading scientific institutions of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine and have different professional expertise will allow enriching the common knowledge and methods of lending, as well as facilitating the interpenetration of tasks and methods between different branches.

This team work of various scientific groups of scientists within the framework of the joint project is the basis for the interdisciplinary KAU Center of Applied Data Science and Innovation (KAU CADSI) at the Kiev Academic University (KAU). Research within the framework of the project will focus on the development, use and dissemination of machine learning and artificial intelligence methods, including methods of Big Data, and application of these methods to various scientific and practical interdisciplinary tasks, in particular, environmental research, in the field of bioinformatics and biomedicine, physical material science, physics and high-energy astrophysics, optimization of individual economic processes, and also in the analysis of innovation activity integrity.
